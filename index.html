What is CalorieMate?

CalorieMate is an innovative mobile app designed to revolutionize how you interact with food. Built as a Final Year Project, it combines advanced technology like OCR (Optical Character Recognition) with health insights to help you track calories, verify safety, and generate recipes. Whether you're managing family meals or comparing products, CalorieMate empowers you to eat smarter, not harder.
